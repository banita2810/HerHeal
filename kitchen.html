PCOS Kitchen Essentials

🥬 Leafy Greens (Fiber)

Recs: Spinach, Kale, Arugula.

Eat these at the start of meals to blunt insulin spikes.

🫐 Berries (Antioxidants)

Recs: Blueberries, Raspberries, Blackberries.

Low-sugar options that won't trigger androgen production.

🫘 Legumes (Slow Carbs)

Recs: Chickpeas, Lentils, Black beans.

High protein and fiber content keeps you full longer.

🥜 Healthy Fats (Hormone building blocks)

Recs: Avocado, Walnuts, Flaxseeds.

Essential for producing progesterone and reducing inflammation.
